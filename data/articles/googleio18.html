It’s about that time again, Google’s developer conference is almost here

By Ali Hussan Ahmed

Google I/O Kicks off today

It’s about that time again, Google’s developer conference is almost here - Like last year they will kick off with another Keynote, this gives Google the chance to talk about all the projects that they’ve been working on for the past year or so.

Last year we had a slew of updates todo with Google Assistant, including Hands Free calling for Google Home and the introduction of Google Assistant on iOS. This Year will have a lot more focus on the Assistant and Chrome OS as well as Wear OS, Android TV, Android Things and Android Auto. A few features for Android Auto has already been released by Google, enhancing what's already a very straightforward and intuitive platform for Cars.

Livestream starts soon

The Livestream of the Keynote will start at 6PM today, check back here later for a link to the stream, or visit the Google I/O website , the livestream is usually on the Google Developers Channel closer to the time.
